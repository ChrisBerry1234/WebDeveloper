Chicken Fried Rice

Home

Chicken Fried Rice is a staple in chinese cuisine

Ingredients Include

    Base

  • 2 cups Cold Cooked Rice
  • 2 Large Eggs
  • 1 cup cooked chicken
  • 2-3 tsp oil

    • Vegetables

  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 garlic cloves, minced

Steps to Follow

  1. Make Sure Rice is cold and separated
  2. Cook the eggs
  3. Cook chicken and veggies
  4. Add the Rice
  5. Finish and Mix Together
