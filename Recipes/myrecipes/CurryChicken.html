Curry Chicken

Home

Curry Chicken is a staple in Jamaican cuisine

Ingredients Include

    Chicken

  • 1.5 Large Large Eggs
  • 1 tsp lemon
  • 1 cup cooked chicken
  • 2-3 tsp oil

    • Marinade

  • 2 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 inch fresh ginger
  • 1 sprig thyme

    • Cooking

  • 1 tsp oil
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1-2 chopped scallions
  • 1-2 potatoes

Steps to Follow

  1. Clean and Marinate
  2. Brown and bloom
  3. Cook the chicken
  4. Simmer
  5. Finish and Serve with White Rice
